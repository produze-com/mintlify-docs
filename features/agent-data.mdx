title: "Agent data access"
---

Below are some of the data sources our Agent has access to.

### **Seller Central**

**Sales**

- Sales and traffic reports

**Inventory**

- FBA inventory reports
- Restock recommendation reports

**PPC**

- Search term reports
- Targeting reports
- Advertised product reports
- SQP reports
- Sponsored rankings

### **Amazon shopper website**

- Product listings
- Bestsellers
- Search results for keywords

### Google sheets

- Use any data from google sheets

### **Keepa**

- Historical pricing data for any ASIN
- Historical BSR Data for any ASIN

### **Jungle Scout**

- Ranking keywords for any ASIN
- Keyword search volume

### **Action Tools**

- Update product listings
- Change product pricing
- Adjust advertising bids
- Create new advertising campaigns
- Block or pause poor performing ad targets

Looking for something, but don't find here? Reach out at [support@superfuel.io](mailto:support@superfuel.io)
