Superfuel is your intelligent Amazon assistant that analyzes data, provides actionable recommendations, and automates critical seller tasks. Think of it as your 24/7 Amazon operations team that never sleeps.

### Why Superfuel?

- **Increase sales by 20%** through reliable and consistent execution of manual tasks in competitor analysis, listing optimization, PPC optimization, and inventory planning
- **Increase profitability by 10%** by optimizing PPC, reducing wasteful spending, and optimizing storage and FBA costs
- **Generate 40% more output** with the same team

### Connected Platforms

- **Seller Central** - Full API access to your account metrics and reports
- **Amazon Shopper Site** - Real-time competitor and market data
- **Google Sheets** - Sync with your custom spreadsheets and data
- **Jungle Scout** - Keyword research and competitor analysis
- **Keepa** - Historical pricing and BSR tracking

### What Can You Do With Superfuel?

- Automate repetitive tasks with your own workflows
- Analyze competitor strategies using real-time market data
- Optimize and monitor listings with data-driven recommendations
- Manage PPC campaigns with intelligent bid adjustments
- Plan inventory using the latest stock levels and sales data
- Generate insights from your Seller Central data

1. **Chat with the Agent** and create any automation you want (screenshot)
2. **Choose from popular automations** used by other brands (screenshot)
